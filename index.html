About me

I am a GIS Technician with the Town of Banff, focused on cartography, spatial data, municipal GIS applications, and clear public-facing maps. My work connects technical GIS systems with the people who rely on them: municipal staff, residents, visitors, council, and project stakeholders.

I build and maintain maps, apps, dashboards, and spatial databases using ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, Experience Builder, Field Maps, StoryMaps, Python, Git, Arcade, R, FME, and Tableau. I enjoy turning complex landscape, infrastructure, traffic, planning, and community datasets into tools that are accurate, useful, and easy to understand.

What I do

  • Cartographic design

    Compelling municipal, planning, 3D, context, and communication maps for public reports and community storytelling.

  • GIS applications

    StoryMaps, Experience Builder apps, Field Maps, web maps, and public map portals with responsive web design built around real user needs.

  • Spatial data ETL

    Automated spatial data workflows using Python, R, Arcade, FME, and SQL, including enterprise data integration, open data publishing, and spatial data governance.

  • Dashboards & analysis

    Interactive dashboards and spatial analysis combining Tableau visualizations and geospatial insights for operational and planning decisions.
